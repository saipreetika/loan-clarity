# LoanClarity 💚

**A free tool to help students understand, plan, and stress less about their education loans.**

https://saipreetika.github.io/loan-clarity/

---

## Why I Built This

I'm an Indian student who moved to the US for my graduate studies, funded by an education loan.

While I was studying, I didn’t think much about it. Fees needed to be paid, the loan covered it, life went on. But as graduation approached and repayment started getting closer, I realised I had no real clarity on:

- What I owed  
- When I had to start paying  
- What would happen if I missed a payment  
- How the numbers actually worked  

I wasn’t alone. Thousands of students across India take education loans every year to study abroad.

Most of us understand the basics *“I borrowed X, I pay it back in Y years.”*  
But nobody really explains the full picture:

- How interest capitalises during the moratorium  
- What a single missed payment actually costs  
- How paying just a little extra each month can save you **lakhs** over the life of the loan  

**LoanClarity is for every student who has ever felt anxious opening their loan documents.**

The goal isn’t to scare you, it’s to replace the anxiety of not knowing with the calm of having a plan.

---

## What It Does

### 📊 Repayment Calculator

Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and how much you plan to pay each month.

Instantly see:

- How long repayment takes  
- How much total interest you'll pay  
- How different EMI amounts compare  

So you can set a real target instead of just paying the minimum.

**Features:**

- Supports any currency (₹, $, £, €, and more)  
- Handles moratorium / grace periods  
- Enter your own PMII amount and duration  
- Accounts for interest capitalisation during the moratorium  
- Shows a full comparison table across 5, 7, 10, 14, and 20-year scenarios  

---

### ⚠️ What If I Miss a Payment?

Enter your EMI, number of missed months, and your bank's penal interest rate.

See the exact financial impact:

- Overdue amount  
- Penal interest  
- Bounce charges  
- Day-by-day escalation breakdown  
- Credit score implications  
- Legal action risk  

Understand how missed payments escalate from a simple fee to something much bigger.

---

### 💡 Survival Tips

Practical, bank-agnostic advice for navigating repayment:

- During your grace period  
- When starting EMIs  
- If facing temporary income gaps  
- If you’re feeling overwhelmed
